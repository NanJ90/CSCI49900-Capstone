## How to read the levels
| &nbsp; | &nbsp; |
| --- | --- |
|Excellent (4)|Exceeds expectations for a strong undergraduate-level project. The kind of work that could be polished into a workshop submission or used as a portfolio piece.|
|Proficient (3)| Solid undergradte-level work. Meets the requirements cleanly with minor gaps. This is the target.|
|Developing (2)|Shows real effort and a working result, but with notable weaknesses in execution, analysis, or communication.|
|Beginning (1)|Significant pieces missing, incorrect, or unclear. Likely needs major revisions to meet course expectations.|
